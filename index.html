Welcome To HappyGirlClub

When you buy a HappyGirlClub NFT, you’re not simply buying an avatar or a provably-rare piece of art. You are gaining membership access to a club whose benefits and offerings will increase over time. Your HappyGirlClub NFT can serve as your digital identity, and open digital doors for you.

THE SPECS

Each HappGirlClub NFT is unique and programmatically generated from over Xno possible backgrounds, eye wear, hairstyle, clothing, and more.
All NFTs are dope, but some are rarer than others. The NFTs are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. (See Record and Proof.)
Purchasing an HappyGirlClub NFT costs 0.001 ETH. To access members-only areas such as THE OG’s, HappyGirlClub holders will need to be signed into their Metamask Wallet.
